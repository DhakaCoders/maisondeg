Spurred by an obsession for accuracy since its creation in 1865, Swiss watchmaker Zenith is renowned for its elegant timepieces that blend noble watchmaking tradition with groundbreaking technical innovation. In 1969, Zenith released the El Primero, which revolutionised the fine watchmaking industry thanks to its exceptionally accurate movement oscillating at over 36,000 vibrations per hour. Today this piece has earned definitive cult status.
